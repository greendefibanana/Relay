# Institutional Workflows

Relay is built for teams that need private coordination before public settlement.

## Founders and Employees

Founders and employees can seek partial liquidity for vested positions without broadcasting a sell signal before terms are agreed.

## Early Investors

Early investors can negotiate secondary transfers for SAFTs, SAFEs, and locked allocations while keeping private deal terms confidential.

## Project Treasuries

Project treasuries can coordinate block sales with qualified buyers or market makers without exposing treasury strategy to public markets.

## OTC Desks

Desks can match private buyers and sellers through RFQs while using Solana settlement as the final execution layer.

## Market Makers

Market makers and projects can coordinate token inventory, treasury distributions, or strategic liquidity transfers with reduced information leakage.

## Issuers

Issuers can preserve transfer controls through clearance and consent flows before a match is allowed to settle.
